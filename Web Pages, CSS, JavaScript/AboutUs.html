1987

This company is originally found by the Margold Brothers,Tim and John. The shop was not called "Pizza Life" at first, but was called "TJ Pizzaplace" before the name change. The two brothers went through some hardships at first, but they have mostly went through it without any issues.

1996

The company is starting to see some uprise in their results. Is was at this time where the brothers decided to sell out about over 65% their share to the public. This was when Sir McMicheal came in and bought out their share and changed the way the organisation works.He is also the one who is in-charged of the name change to "Pizza Time".

Now

Currently,"Pizza Life" is well-known around the globe and is ranked at the top of the pizza place rankings.We currently have around 60+ retail stores throughout the globe and we are planning to increase it again for the next coming years."Pizza Time" is famous for our hand-made pizzas and side-dishes.It was also around this time,where we decided to enchance our business capabilities by creating a webpage for our company to help and improve our non-inhouse business and let others enjoy our products even without coming into our local stores.
